1. Open the image in commons.wikimedia.org

2. Click the <Use this file> on the web link. 

3. Copy the contents of the attribution field. EG:

By Brian Stansberry (Own work) [CC-BY-3.0 (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0)], via Wikimedia Commons

4. Turn this into a minimal attribution. EG:

Brian Stansberry / <a href="http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/" >CC-BY-3.0</a>
